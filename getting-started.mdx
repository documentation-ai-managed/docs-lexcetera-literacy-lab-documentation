## Overview

Literacy Lab empowers adult learners with career skills and multiliteracy development through interactive modules, personalized paths, and real-world projects. Follow these steps to create your account, configure your dashboard, and explore your first resources. You'll be mastering new skills in under 10 minutes.

<Callout kind="tip">

Prepare your email address and a quiet space. The platform works on desktop and mobile browsers for flexible learning.

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up in moments to access personalized learning recommendations.

<Steps>

<Step title="Visit Literacy Lab" icon="globe">

Navigate to `https://app.lexcetera.com` and click **Sign Up**.

</Step>

<Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">

Provide your email, create a strong password, and select your primary goal:

- Career advancement
- Digital multiliteracy
- Basic skills refresh

Confirm via the email link sent to your inbox.

</Step>

<Step title="Complete Profile" icon="edit-3">

Answer 3 quick questions about your experience level and interests. This unlocks tailored content.

</Step>

</Steps>

## Set Up Your Dashboard

After login, customize your dashboard for efficient navigation.

<Tabs>

<Tab title="Career Skills Focus" icon="briefcase">

1. Select **Career Path** from the top menu.

2. Choose a track like Resume Building or Interview Prep.

3. Enable daily goal notifications in **Settings > Notifications**.

</Tab>

<Tab title="Multiliteracy Development" icon="book-open">

1. Click **Literacy Hub** on the dashboard.

2. Pick a module such as Digital Navigation or Critical Media Analysis.

3. Set progress tracking preferences under **Profile > Goals**.

</Tab>

</Tabs>

## Integrate with Your Workflow

For educators or developers, embed Literacy Lab modules using simple code.

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,HTML">

```javascript
// Embed a module in your app
const embedModule = (moduleId) => {
  const script = document.createElement('script');
  script.src = `https://app.lexcetera.com/embed.js?module=${moduleId}&key=YOUR_API_KEY`;
  document.body.appendChild(script);
};

embedModule('digital-literacy-101');
```

```html
<!-- Simple iframe embed -->
<iframe
  src="https://app.lexcetera.com/embed/resume-builder?token=YOUR_TOKEN"
  width="100%"
  height="600"
  frameborder="0">
</iframe>
```

</CodeGroup>
